Elias

The "Anxious Avoider"

Elias is a 23-year-old recent graduate carrying $45,000 in student debt and struggling with high-interest credit card balances. Frustrated and anxious about his finances, he avoids checking his accounts but deeply wants financial stability and independence, especially the ability to rent an apartment without relying on a cosigner.

Goals

  • Move into an apartment without a cosigner
  • Improve his credit score safely
  • Pay down high-interest credit card debt

Frustrations

  • Watching his credit score fluctuate
  • High-interest credit card debt (24% APR)
  • Lack of financial education growing up
